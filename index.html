About Me

I’m a healthcare professional who has always been curious about the “why” behind the work. What started with claims, billing, and patient coordination gradually grew into a passion for uncovering patterns, solving problems, and building smarter ways to connect people with care.

Over time, I’ve developed a toolkit that blends healthcare know-how with technical skills. From analyzing data in Python and SQL to bringing insights to life in Power BI. Just as important, I’ve learned how to translate those insights into solutions that are practical, scalable, and designed with people in mind.

At the core, I believe healthcare is about impact. For me, data isn’t just rows and columns, it’s the story of how we can make systems more efficient, reduce frustration for patients, and give providers better tools to do what they do best. That’s the bridge I’m building: turning healthcare experience and informatics into meaningful change.
